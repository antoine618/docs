## Creating Your Account

When you first sign up for Moonrank, you'll be guided through our onboarding wizard:

1. **Enter Your Website URL** - This is where your articles will be published
2. **Verify Your Site** - Add a DNS record or upload an HTML file to verify ownership
3. **Configure Your CMS** - Connect WordPress, Webflow, or manual publishing
4. **Set Your Goals** - Define what success looks like (traffic targets, ranking positions, etc.)

## Dashboard Preferences

Configure how Moonrank displays information in your **Settings → Dashboard Preferences**:

- **Default Time Range** - Choose between 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or custom date ranges
- **Notification Frequency** - Get daily, weekly, or monthly summaries
- **Content Language** - Select your preferred language for article generation
- **Timezone** - Set your timezone for accurate scheduling

## CMS Integration

### WordPress Integration

- Install the Moonrank plugin from your WordPress dashboard
- Authenticate with your Moonrank account
- Articles will auto-publish as drafts for your review

### Manual Publishing

- Generate articles in Moonrank
- Copy the content and publish manually to your site
- Update the article URL in Moonrank to track performance

### API Integration

- Use Moonrank's API to automate publishing
- See API Reference section for detailed documentation

## Team Management

### Add Team Members

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role: Admin, Editor, or Viewer

### Role Permissions

- **Admin** - Full access to all features and settings
- **Editor** - Can create, edit, and publish articles; cannot access settings
- **Viewer** - Read-only access to reports and analytics

## Billing & Subscription

### View Your Plan

- Navigate to **Settings → Billing**
- See your current subscription tier
- View next billing date and cost

### Upgrade or Downgrade

- Click "Change Plan" to modify your subscription
- Changes take effect at your next billing cycle
- Downgrading within 30 days qualifies for a prorated refund

### Manage Payment Method

- Update your credit card or billing information
- Set up invoice email delivery
- Export billing history

## Two-Factor Authentication

Protect your account with 2FA:

1. Go to **Settings → Security**
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

## Email Preferences

Control what emails you receive:

1. **Settings → Email Preferences**
2. Toggle specific email types on/off:
   - Daily ranking updates
   - Article performance alerts
   - Weekly summaries
   - Technical audit reports
   - System notifications
