Follow these essential steps to start using Moonrank and create your first high-ranking article.

## Step 1: Set Up Your Account

1. Navigate to your dashboard after signing in
2. Click "Account Settings" in the left menu
3. Enter your website URL and target keywords
4. Configure your publishing preferences (manual or auto-publish)
5. Click "Save Settings"

## Step 2: Create Your First Article

1. Go to **Articles** â†’ **Create New Article**
2. Enter your target keyword or topic
3. Let Moonrank analyze search intent and competitor content
4. Review the generated article outline
5. Customize the content as needed
6. Click "Preview" to see how it will look
7. Publish to your site or schedule for later

## Step 3: Track Your Keywords

1. Navigate to **Keywords** section
2. Click "Add Keywords"
3. Enter 5-10 keywords you want to rank for
4. Set your target positions
5. Enable "Track Rankings" to monitor progress
6. Moonrank will check positions daily

## Step 4: Run a Technical Audit

1. Go to **Technical Audit**
2. Click "Scan Website"
3. Wait for the audit to complete (usually 5-10 minutes)
4. Review identified issues by priority (Critical, Warning, Info)
5. Click on each issue to see recommended fixes
6. Fix issues on your website

## Step 5: Monitor Your Results

1. Visit your **Dashboard** daily
2. Check Article Performance cards
3. Review your Keyword Rankings
4. Monitor your Technical Health score
