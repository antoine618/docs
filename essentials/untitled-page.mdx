## Creating Your Content Plan
### Step 1: Define Your Goals
1. Go to **Content Plan → Settings**
2. Set your content goals:
   - Monthly article target (e.g., publish 4 articles/month)
   - Total traffic goal
   - Keyword ranking targets
3. Save your goals
### Step 2: Select Your Keywords
1. Go to **Content Plan → Keywords**
2. Import or select keywords you want to rank for
3. Moonrank calculates estimated effort (time to rank)
4. Set priority for each keyword:
   - High (target within 30 days)
   - Medium (target within 90 days)
   - Low (target within 180 days)
### Step 3: Generate Article Ideas
1. Click "Generate Ideas"
2. For each keyword, Moonrank suggests:
   - Article outline
   - Estimated word count
   - Related topics to link to
   - Content angle unique to your audience
### Step 4: Schedule Your Content
1. Drag articles into your calendar
2. Schedule publish dates
3. Assign team members
4. Set review deadlines
5. Save your plan
## Calendar View
The Content Plan calendar shows:
- **Color coding** - Articles by status (Draft, Scheduled, Published)
- **Multiple views** - Weekly, monthly, or quarterly
- **Drag & drop** - Reschedule articles easily
- **Capacity view** - How many articles per team member per week
## Content Pipeline
Track the status of all planned content:
### Planning Stage
- Ideas generated
- Keywords assigned
- Outlines approved
### Creation Stage
- Draft being written
- Content in editor
- Optimization in progress
### Review Stage
- Submitted for approval
- Feedback given
- Revisions in progress
### Publishing Stage
- Scheduled for publication
- Published (live)
- Monitoring performance
## Collaboration & Assignments
### Assign Articles
1. Click on a planned article
2. Select "Assign to" team member
3. Set deadline
4. Team member gets notification
### Comments & Feedback
- Team members comment on articles during review
- Tagged comments notify specific people
- Resolved discussions keep threads organized
### Approval Workflows
Set up approval requirements:
- Editor writes
- Manager reviews
- Admin publishes
## Performance Tracking
### Monitor Your Plan Progress
The dashboard shows:
- Articles published vs. planned
- Content calendar completion percentage
- Which articles are driving traffic
- Keyword ranking improvements
### Adjustment & Iteration
If articles underperform:
- Identify why (poor keyword choice, weak content, bad title)
- Update the article and republish
- Track if changes improve performance
- Learn for future content
## Content Ideas Marketplace
Browse AI-generated ideas for your industry:
1. Go to **Content Plan → Ideas**
2. Filter by topic, difficulty, or search volume
3. Click "Add to Plan" to schedule
4. Customize for your brand voice
5. Publish when ready
