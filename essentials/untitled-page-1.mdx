## Dashboard Overview
The main dashboard shows your key metrics at a glance:
### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Organic Traffic** - Total visitors from Google and other search engines
- **Avg. Position** - Your average ranking position across all tracked keywords
- **Keywords Ranking** - How many keywords rank in top 10, 20, 100
- **Articles Published** - Total content published in selected time period
- **Indexed Pages** - Pages Google has in its index
## Traffic Analytics
### Traffic Sources
See where your visitors come from:
- **Organic Search** - From Google and search engines
- **Direct** - Visitors typing your URL
- **Referral** - From links on other sites
- **Social** - From social media platforms
### Traffic Trends
- View traffic over time (day, week, month, year)
- Compare periods to track growth
- Identify seasonal patterns
- Spot traffic anomalies
### Top Performing Articles
- Which articles drive the most traffic
- Traffic trend for each article (up, down, stable)
- Keywords each article ranks for
- Estimated monthly organic traffic per article
## Keyword Ranking Metrics
### Ranking Distribution
- How many keywords rank in positions 1-3 (high value)
- Positions 4-10 (good visibility)
- Positions 11-20 (room for improvement)
- Position 21+ (needs optimization)
### Ranking Changes
Track how your rankings change:
- Keywords improving (going up in position)
- Keywords declining (going down in position)
- New keywords ranking for first time
- Keywords fell out of top 100
### Visibility Score
Moonrank's proprietary metric showing your total estimated search visibility:
- Accounts for ranking position AND search volume
- Higher visibility = more clicks
- Track if your total visibility is growing or shrinking
## Article Performance
### Individual Article Metrics
For each published article:
- Total views
- Estimated organic traffic
- Keyword ranking for target keyword
- Engagement (time on page, bounce rate)
- Backlinks pointing to the article
### Article ROI
Calculate return on investment:
- Estimated content creation time
- Estimated traffic value (based on industry benchmarks)
- When the article paid for itself
- Total lifetime value
## Content Performance Comparison
### Compare Articles
- Side-by-side article performance
- Identify what makes top performers successful
- Apply lessons to future content
### Benchmark Against Competitors
- How your articles compare to competitors
- Your share of traffic for key keywords
- Opportunities to improve visibility
